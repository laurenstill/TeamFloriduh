Health Datapalooza 2014

Working with the 2012 CMS Medicare Fee-for-Service Provider Utilization and Payment Data and identifying care voids/deserts for common medicare patient homes. Initial statistical analysis was completed in R with python to build out heat maps for better population health management.


The analysis singled out several case scenarios starting with a national picture of neurology, nephrology and cardiology joins, representing a patient with a history of stroke, kidney and heart disease. Mirrors population pretty well (as expected). The project also explored family diads and multiple patient comorbidities within specific geographic locations.
